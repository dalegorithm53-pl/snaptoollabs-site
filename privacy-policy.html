Local Processing

üí°

Our core tools ‚Äî including OCR (optical character recognition), PDF manipulation, and document conversion ‚Äî operate entirely within your browser using client-side JavaScript and WebAssembly. No document content ever leaves your device.

This architecture means:

  • Files you open or drag into our extensions are read by your local browser ‚Äî not uploaded to any server.
  • Extracted text, converted files, and processed output are generated and stored locally in your browser session.
  • Closing the extension or clearing your browser data removes any locally cached content.
  • We have no technical capability to access documents you process, because they never reach our infrastructure.

Where cloud-assisted features are offered in the future (e.g., sync or backup), they will be clearly labeled, opt-in, and described separately in an updated policy.

We use the limited information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Services: authenticating accounts, managing subscriptions, and delivering features.

  • Communications: sending transactional emails (account confirmation, receipts) and, with your consent, product updates. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making that has legal or similarly significant effects on you.

  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: we do not process sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to provide the Services.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: we will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.

Submitting a CCPA Request

To submit a verifiable consumer request, email snap.tool.labs@gmail.com with the subject line "CCPA Request." We will respond within 45 days. You may submit up to two requests per 12-month period at no charge.
